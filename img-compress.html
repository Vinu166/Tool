Frequently Asked Questions

Will compression affect my image quality?

Compression always involves some quality loss, but our tool intelligently balances size reduction and visual quality. By adjusting the compression level, you can control how much quality you're willing to sacrifice for smaller file sizes.

What's the maximum file size I can upload?

Our tool supports images up to 10MB in size. For larger files, you may need to resize the image first or use a desktop application.

Is my data safe when using this tool?

Yes! All compression happens directly in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring complete privacy and security of your data.

Which image formats are supported?

Our tool supports common image formats including JPEG, PNG, GIF, and WebP. You can also convert between formats during compression.

What compression method is used?

We use a combination of techniques including optimized encoding parameters, quality reduction, and metadata removal. For transparent images like PNGs, we utilize specialized methods to maintain transparency while reducing file size.
